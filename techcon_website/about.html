The TechCon Evolution

TechCon started in 2018 with a simple goal: to create a hub for tech enthusiasts in Nairobi. Since then, it's grown into Africa's leading technology event, drawing thousands and top minds worldwide. Our journey has been about constantly adapting to the fast-paced tech world and building a strong community of innovators. From our beginnings, TechCon has consistently been a place for big ideas and teamwork, proving its importance in Africa's tech development.

Our Core Purpose

At TechCon, we aim to **empower, connect, and inspire**. We're dedicated to creating an environment where cutting-edge knowledge is shared, new ideas take flight, and valuable connections are made. Our main beliefs focus on boosting tech literacy, driving sustainable growth through technology, and showing Africa's huge potential as a global tech leader. We believe technology can change lives and economies, and we strive to be the force behind that change by bringing together diverse perspectives and expertise.

Highlighted Contributors

Dr. Aisha Khan (2021)

Dr. Aisha Khan is a respected AI ethicist, led the way in ethical AI. Her 2021 talk, "AI for Good: Navigating the Ethical Frontier," made attendees think about AI's impact on society and pushed for inclusive AI design. Her work continues to shape global discussions on tech and human rights.

Mark Owusu (2019)

Mark Owusu, a cybersecurity expert and founder of SecureNet Africa, gave a powerful talk in 2019 on "Fortifying Africa's Digital Landscape." His insights on new cyber threats and defense strategies were crucial, making him a key voice in cybersecurity resilience across Africa.

Nia Imani (2023)

Nia Imani, a leader in sustainable technology, shared her vision for a greener future in 2023. Her session, "Innovating for a Sustainable Planet," highlighted groundbreaking eco-friendly tech solutions and encouraged many participants to consider environmental impact in their tech work.
