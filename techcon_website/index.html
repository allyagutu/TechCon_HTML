Our History

TechCon began in 2018 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Nairobi, driven by the vision to create a platform for African innovation. Over the years, it has grown into the continent's largest technology conference, attracting thousands of attendees and influential figures from around the globe. Our journey has been marked by continuous evolution, adapting to the dynamic tech landscape and fostering a vibrant community of innovators. From humble beginnings, TechCon has consistently provided a stage for groundbreaking ideas and collaborative opportunities, solidifying its reputation as a cornerstone of technological advancement in Africa.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower, connect, and inspire. We are dedicated to fostering a collaborative environment where cutting-edge knowledge is shared, innovative ideas are sparked, and meaningful connections are forged. Our core principles revolve around promoting technological literacy, driving sustainable development through tech, and showcasing Africa's immense potential as a global tech hub. We believe in the power of technology to transform lives and economies, and we strive to be the catalyst for that change by bringing together diverse perspectives and expertise.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Aisha Khan (TechCon 2021)

Dr. Aisha Khan is a renowned AI ethicist and pioneer in responsible AI development. Her keynote at TechCon 2021, "AI for Good: Navigating the Ethical Frontier," challenged attendees to consider the societal implications of artificial intelligence and advocated for inclusive AI design. Her work continues to influence global conversations on technology and human rights.

Mark Owusu (TechCon 2019)

Mark Owusu, a cybersecurity expert and founder of SecureNet Africa, delivered a compelling presentation at TechCon 2019 on "Fortifying Africa's Digital Landscape." His insights into emerging cyber threats and practical defense strategies were invaluable, establishing him as a leading voice in cybersecurity resilience across the continent.

Nia Imani (TechCon 2023)

Nia Imani, a leading voice in sustainable technology, shared her vision for a greener future at TechCon 2023. Her session, "Innovating for a Sustainable Planet," highlighted groundbreaking eco-friendly tech solutions and inspired countless participants to integrate environmental consciousness into their technological pursuits.
